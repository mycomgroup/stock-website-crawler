# EODHD Becomes Official Deutsche Börse Market Data Distributor

## 源URL

https://eodhd.com/financial-apis-blog/eodhd-becomes-official-deutsche-borse-market-data-distributor

## 正文

We are excited to announce that EODHD APIs has become an official authorized distributor of Deutsche Börse market data. This partnership marks an important step in expanding our European market data coverage and bringing institutional-grade German exchange data to EODHD users worldwide.

Deutsche Börse Group is one of the largest exchange organizations in the world, operating the Frankfurt Stock Exchange and the Xetra electronic trading platform. Xetra is the reference market for German equities and ETFs, handling over 90% of all exchange trading in German shares. It is the home of the DAX index — Germany’s benchmark stock index – and hosts trading for thousands of securities including blue-chip equities, ETFs, bonds, and structured products.

We will announce specific API endpoints and availability dates as the integration progresses. Stay tuned for updates.

For EODHD users building European-focused strategies, backtesting models on German equities, or tracking DAX-listed companies, this partnership means direct access to one of Europe’s most important market data sources. Combined with our existing coverage of 70+ exchanges globally, this further strengthens EODHD as a comprehensive financial data platform.

If you have specific requirements for Deutsche Börse data, feel free to reach out to our support team – your feedback helps us prioritize the integration roadmap.
