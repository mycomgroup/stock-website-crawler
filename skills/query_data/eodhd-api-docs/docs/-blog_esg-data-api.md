# ESG Data API

## 源URL

## 正文

We are excited to welcome a new addition to our marketplace: the ESG data package by our partner, InvestVerte.

ESG data refers to information that measures a company’s performance across Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) criteria. This ESG database provides insights into how companies manage their environmental impact (such as carbon emissions and energy efficiency), social factors (like labor practices and diversity), and governance practices (such as board structure and executive pay). Investors and analysts use ESG data analysis to assess sustainability, define ESG risks, and align investment strategies with long-term growth. Understanding the meaning of ESG and ESG data definition is crucial for meeting regulatory requirements and achieving transparency.

InvestVerte’s ESG data API is a holistic solution tailored for those seeking reliable and transparent ESG data. With advanced ESG data analytics, it offers real-time updates, data collection, and detailed company profiles, making it ideal for those looking to integrate datastream ESG into their investment strategies.

Our ESG data extraction capabilities provide in-depth ESG ratings and ESG data analysis, helping you stay ahead in a competitive market. Real-time updates and tailored insights make it easy to adapt to market trends and ESG data analytics needs.

Leverage InvestVerte’s ESG data base to drive your business toward sustainable growth. With ESG and data analytics, you’ll gain valuable insights and unlock the potential of ESG data analytics courses to stay ahead in the market.
