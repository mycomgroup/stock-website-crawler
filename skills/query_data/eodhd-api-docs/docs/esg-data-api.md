## 产品描述

ESG Data by InvestVerte gives you comparable Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) scores and benchmarks across companies, countries, and sectors - now enhanced with an AI-based scoring and intelligence framework.

What’s new (AI upgrade)

AI & Deep Learning–based ESG completion: missing or inconsistent ESG datapoints are estimated using peer patterns at country/sector/sub-sector level, with a conservative approach for limited disclosures.
Dedicated AI models per pillar (E/S/G):
Environmental: emissions, energy, resources, waste, climate exposure
Social: workforce, H&S, diversity, human rights, supply chain, stakeholder disclosures
Governance: board structure, pay alignment, shareholder rights, risk & ethics controls
Context-aware aggregation: a higher-level model combines E, S, and G while accounting for regulatory, sector, and geographic context - so results remain globally comparable and locally relevant.

What you get via API

Company ESG ratings (E, S, G, total ESG), by year and frequency (FY/Q1/Q2/Q3/Q4)
Country ESG statistics (mean/median), by year and frequency
Sector benchmarks and sector lists
