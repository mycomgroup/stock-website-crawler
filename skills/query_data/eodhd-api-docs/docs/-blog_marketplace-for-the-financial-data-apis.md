# Marketplace for the Financial Data APIs

## 源URL

https://eodhd.com/financial-apis-blog/marketplace-for-the-financial-data-apis

## 正文

We are excited to announce the launch of our financial data marketplace, the platform for financial data APIs. This marketplace is not just a repository of data; it is a foundation for growth and discovery in the financial sector, recently initiated and constantly evolving with invaluable feedback from users like you.

Our marketplace is currently showcasing an array of advanced technical APIs, courtesy of our data vendor ‘Robexia‘. We’re beginning our journey with a strong emphasis on providing sophisticated technical indicators. Users can now integrate APIs offering Ichimoku Clouds, Candlestick Patterns, and a plethora of other technical indicators, each designed to provide a granular analysis of market trends and potential trading opportunities.

We believe our marketplace will be an indispensable ally for all our users and potential data providers. We’re here to support your ventures with high-quality, technical financial data that is crucial for informed decision-making and strategy development. Your feedback is the cornerstone upon which we will build a more robust and user-centric marketplace.

As we move forward, we invite you to be an integral part of this journey, to explore the capabilities of our APIs, and to share your experiences and insights with us. Your voice will help shape a marketplace that truly reflects the needs and ambitions of the financial community.

You can check our new APIs in the Financial Data Marketplace.

Stay tuned.
