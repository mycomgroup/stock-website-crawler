# Meet PRAAMS: 4 Game-Changing Financial Intelligence Tools Now Live on Our Marketplace

## 源URL

https://eodhd.com/financial-apis-blog/meet-praams-4-game-changing-financial-intelligence-tools-now-live-on-our-marketplace

## 正文

We’re thrilled to welcome PRAAMS, an advanced provider of financial intelligence, to our Marketplace! With a focus on deep analytics, rapid insights, and professional-grade outputs, PRAAMS offers four exceptional APIs covering equities, bonds, and banks across the globe. Whether you’re building fintech apps, powering research dashboards, or need automated due diligence – PRAAMS delivers.

What it does:PRAAMS Analyse API offers lightning-fast risk and return analysis for over 120,000+ global equities and bonds. Input a stock ticker or bond ISIN and receive an expertly curated summary of the asset’s performance, risk profile, and valuation.

Why it matters:No need for manual digging through ratios or spreadsheets. The PRAAMS Ratio, backed by 470+ professional metrics, instantly summarizes an asset’s attractiveness.

Highlights:

Perfect for: Asset managers, risk teams, quant developers

What it does:Tired of generic financials for banks? PRAAMS Bank Financials API provides purpose-built statements tailored for banking analysis – including core revenue, net interest income, IBPT, provisions, and more.

Why it matters:Built by experienced CFA-level bank analysts, this API covers all major global and regional banks in the format analysts actually need.

Highlights:

Perfect for: Financial institutions, regulators, credit risk teams

What it does:Need trade ideas? The Explore API allows you to screen 120,000+ instruments using 12+ smart risk-return filters, from dividend strength to volatility to analyst sentiment.

Why it matters:Build screeners like: “Undervalued Chinese tech stocks with high dividends” or  “High-yield European bank bonds with low default risk”.

Highlights:

Perfect for: Investment strategists, fund screeners, quant developers

What it does:Generate multi-page PDF reports for every equity or bond covered by PRAAMS – updated daily. Each PDF includes a tailored analysis with visuals, return and risk metrics, and brief commentary.

Why it matters:Need a report to share with clients, teammates, or read offline? It’s ready in seconds.

Highlights:

Perfect for: Wealth advisors, client presentations, compliance archiving

These APIs are plug-and-play and available right now on the Marketplace. Whether you’re building algo strategies, automating investment research, or powering credit models – PRAAMS helps you move faster and smarter.
