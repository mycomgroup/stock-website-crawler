# Intraday API. 1-minute intervals for the past year

## 源URL

https://eodhd.com/financial-apis-blog/intraday-api-1-minute-intervals-for-the-past-year

## 正文

Today we are happy to announce more data for our Intraday Data API. In addition to the current 5-minutes API, we added much more data for US tickers traded on NYSE and NASDAQ exchanges. The new data is much more comprehensive and has several highlights:

The support of pre-market and after-hours or extended hours means that in addition to the main session data from 9:00 am to 4:30 pm EST we also have extended trading data. Pre-market runs between 4:00 am and 9:30 am EST and after-hours trading runs from 4:00 pm to 8:00 pm EST.

Please note, the lower volume in extended hours can lead to increased risk and volatility, although this can also present opportunities for the astute trader.

Enjoy! More information you can get in our Intraday Data API.
