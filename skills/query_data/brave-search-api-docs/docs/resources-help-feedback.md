Account

A verification email is sent during registration and each time you login. Check spam or junk
folders, as these messages can sometimes be filtered. If the email is still not received,
contact support from the email address used during registration so the issue can be
investigated.

This error typically occurs when special characters (such as &, ', or .) are entered in the
company or name fields during registration. Remove any special characters from these fields and
try again.

Navigate to the Billing section under Settings
and click “Update Payment Method”. You’ll be redirected to a Stripe page where you can enter your
new payment details.

Send us an email from the email address associated with the Brave Search API account you’d
like to delete.

Subscriptions

Yes. Custom plans are available for high-volume usage or specialized requirements.
Contact us via our custom plan form.

To make sure you pick the right plan, identify your core needs and goals. The Search plan
covers web, news, image, and video search with local enrichments, rich data and LLM context for LLMs and agents. The Answers plan adds AI-powered answers grounded on a single search or multiple searches for better accuracy & reduced hallucinations. Both plans
include recurring monthly credits to get you started. For autocomplete and spelling correction,
the Autosuggest and Spellcheck plans are available separately.

If your company needs a bespoke, large-data solution, please contact searchapi-support@brave.com.

The credit card serves as an anti-fraud measure to protect Brave from bad actors. It is also
used to process charges for any usage beyond your included monthly credits.

Yes. After you’ve subscribed to a plan, you will need to generate a new key in the API Keys
section - this applies to all plans. You may have up to 3 keys per subscription.

Once your included monthly credits are used up, additional usage is charged on a
pay-as-you-go basis. You can set spending limits in your dashboard to control costs. Usage and remaining credits are visible in the dashboard at any time.

Data retention rights depend on your plan. Please refer to our Terms of Service for details on what data you may
retain from API responses. If you have specific data storage needs, please contact searchapi-support@brave.com.

Billing

Certain cards may not authorize successfully with our payment flow. The decline can come from
either the card issuer or payment processor and doesn’t always provide a detailed error message.

Common causes include:

Insufficient funds (minimum $1 required for authorization)

Card restrictions on international or recurring transactions (commonly occurs with Indian bank cards)

Authentication failures during the authorization process

Recommended solutions:

Try a different Visa or Mastercard credit or debit card, which works reliably with our authorization flow

Sign up for the Brave Search API through our AWS Marketplace listing, which supports additional payment methods (Paid only)

Search Index

The Brave Search API is powered by Brave Search, a fully independent search engine serving
tens of millions of queries per day.

Try Brave Search at search.brave.com.

Yes, every search engine that has its own index necessarily has its own Web crawler. The Brave
Search crawler does not advertise a differentiated user-agent because we must avoid
discrimination from websites that allow only Google to crawl them. However, if a domain or
page is not crawlable by any search engine (it has a noindex tag), or if it is not crawlable
by googlebot, then Brave Search’s bot will not crawl it either.

Brave Search’s crawler is partially powered by information provided by users enrolled in the
Web Discovery Project (WDP) option in Brave browser’s search settings, which is an
off-by-default (aka opt-in), privacy-preserving system. WDP has multiple mechanisms to prevent
Brave from knowing who is contributing what (WDP is also open-source for auditing and inspection by anyone).

Yes. Brave Search fetches millions of web pages every day, offering extensive coverage of
recent events around the world.
